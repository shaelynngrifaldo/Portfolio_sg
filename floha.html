Internship Overview

During my internship, I designed multiple identification guides to support local conservation outreach initiatives. These materials combined field photography, ecological research, and visual design to enhance public understanding of native species.

Wildflower Identification Guide

Field guide highlighting native wildflowers along the Lower Haw River, including habitat notes and identification tips.

Flowering Shrubs & Trees Guide

Educational guide introducing native shrubs and trees, emphasizing ecological functions and seasonal characteristics.

Snake Identification Guide

Informational pamphlet focused on identifying local snake species and distinguishing venomous from non-venomous varieties.
